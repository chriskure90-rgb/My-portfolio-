Instructional Tool • 2024

Guided Self-Explanation for Classroom Discussion

A structured support tool designed to enhance student participation in discussions, heavily grounded in ICAP theory. By guiding learners through four stages of self-explanation—summarizing concepts, linking to prior knowledge, identifying gaps, and clarifying opinions—the tool pushes students into highly constructive and interactive modes of cognitive engagement. Targeted feedback and hints encourage deep exploration rather than providing direct answers.

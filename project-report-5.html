Discuss Ready: Guided Preparation for Classroom Discussion

An interactive app grounded in ICAP theory that helps students deeply understand class material, practice with AI agents, and confidently prepare scripts for live discussions.

Duration 3 months
Role Instructional Designer
Topic EdTech / Cognitive Engagement

Project Overview

Discuss Ready is an instructional application designed to transform how students prepare for upcoming classroom discussions. Rather than passively reading assigned materials, students use the app to actively process concepts, practice articulating their thoughts with AI agents, and draft tangible scripts that they can directly share in class. The entire experience is heavily grounded in the ICAP theory of cognitive engagement.

Problem Statement & Context

Many students struggle to participate in live classroom discussions. This often stems from a lack of deep foundational understanding of the reading materials, compounded by the anxiety of formulating arguments on the spot.

Key Challenge

Without structured scaffolding, students often remain in the "Passive" or "Active" stages of cognitive engagement (simply reading or highlighting text), which does not adequately prepare them for the highly "Interactive" demands of a live classroom debate or discussion.

Approach & Methodology

To bridge the gap between solitary study and live participation, Discuss Ready guides students through a structured learning path designed to elevate their cognitive engagement into the "Constructive" and "Interactive" modes of the ICAP framework:

1

Deep Understanding (Constructive)

Students are guided through structured prompts to summarize concepts, link new information to prior knowledge, and identify gaps in their understanding. This ensures they go beyond surface-level reading.

2

Practice with AI Agents (Interactive)

Students test their ideas by conversing with an AI agent programmed to act as a peer or a Socratic tutor. The AI provides targeted feedback and hints, challenging students to defend their viewpoints and clarify their opinions in a low-stakes environment.

3

Script Preparation

As a final deliverable, the app helps students synthesize their learning and AI practice into useful, concrete scripts and sentence starters. They can bring these directly into the classroom to confidently share their thoughts.
